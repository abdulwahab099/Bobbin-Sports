About Us

BOBBIN SPORTS is a sportswear manufacturer based in Sialkot, Pakistan — a city with a proud legacy in sports goods manufacturing. We combine local craftsmanship with strict quality checks to deliver export-grade apparel. Our focus is on fit, durability, and performance so teams and athletes get gear they can trust.

We work with clubs, schools, and retailers to provide custom sizes, branding, and bulk production. From sample approval to full production runs, our process is designed to be simple, reliable, and export-compliant.
